About Salon de PET-LEEN

Since 1995, Salon de PET-LEEN has been Margao's premier destination for luxury beauty and wellness. We believe in empowering our clients through expert care in a relaxing, elegant environment. Our team prides itself on continuous learning, organic treatments, and delivering masterful artistry—from bridal makeovers to advanced skin therapy.

What We Look For

  • Passion & Skill: A genuine love for the beauty industry and a desire to perfect your craft.
  • Professionalism: Excellent communication skills and a warm, inviting demeanor with clients.
  • Team Spirit: A collaborative attitude to ensure every client leaves feeling radiant.
